## Skills by Phase

### Phase 1 ‚Äî Opportunity and Portfolio Framing
- `artefact-authoring` ‚Äî create Opportunity Statement, Value Hypothesis, Feasibility Notes
- `risk-management` ‚Äî build initial risk register in subfase 1.2
- `ai-lifecycle` ‚Äî assess AI/data feasibility and justify the AI approach
- `gate-checklist` ‚Äî prepare Gate A evidence review

### Phase 2 ‚Äî Inception and Product Framing
- `phase-contract` ‚Äî create the Phase 2 contract; validate entry criteria
- `artefact-authoring` ‚Äî produce Product Vision, Architecture Pack, Roadmap
- `risk-management` ‚Äî update risk register with Phase 2 risks
- `lifecycle-tailoring` ‚Äî adjust framework depth for product type
- `responsibility-flows` ‚Äî produce Role-Responsibility Map
- `gate-checklist` ‚Äî prepare Gate B evidence review

### Phase 3 ‚Äî Discovery and Backlog Readiness
- `backlog-management` ‚Äî shape requirements and prioritize backlog
- `definition-of-done` ‚Äî agree DoD at story and sprint level
- `ai-lifecycle` ‚Äî define AI backlog items and assess data readiness
- `artefact-authoring` ‚Äî fill Discovery Findings and Acceptance Criteria Catalog
- `evidence-management` ‚Äî assemble Gate C evidence pack
- `gate-checklist` ‚Äî conduct Gate C review

### Phase 4 ‚Äî Iterative Delivery and Continuous Validation
- `sprint-facilitation` ‚Äî facilitate sprint planning, standup, review, and retrospective
- `definition-of-done` ‚Äî verify story-level and sprint-level DoD each iteration
- `ai-lifecycle` ‚Äî run AI delivery loop (experiments, evaluation, model iteration)
- `metrics-tracking` ‚Äî record sprint metrics and track threshold breaches
- `risk-management` ‚Äî update risks and clarifications across delivery sprints
- `change-control` ‚Äî evaluate and process scope or architecture changes
- `phase-contract-enforcement` ‚Äî track deviations from the phase contract
- `gate-checklist` ‚Äî prepare Gate D release authorization evidence

### Phase 5 ‚Äî Release, Rollout and Transition
- `operational-readiness` ‚Äî assess readiness and plan operational transition before deployment
- `gate-checklist` ‚Äî conduct Gate E operations handover review
- `metrics-tracking` ‚Äî establish SLO baseline and monitor during hypercare
- `risk-management` ‚Äî track and close hypercare incidents and residual risks

### Phase 6 ‚Äî Operations, Measurement and Improvement
- `metrics-tracking` ‚Äî record service metrics, product analytics, and AI monitoring data
- `ai-lifecycle` ‚Äî manage model drift, retraining triggers, and AI ops
- `retrospective` ‚Äî facilitate continuous improvement review cycles
- `change-control` ‚Äî evaluate and raise change recommendations
- `gate-checklist` ‚Äî prepare Gate F governance review package

### Phase 7 ‚Äî Retire or Replace
- `artefact-authoring` ‚Äî produce Retirement Decision Record, Sunset Plan, Final Closure Pack
- `risk-management` ‚Äî close all open risks before decommissioning
- `metrics-tracking` ‚Äî produce final product performance summary for closure pack
