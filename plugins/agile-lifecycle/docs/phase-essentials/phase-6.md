# Phase 6 — Operations, Measurement and Improvement Essentials

## What to Do
- Run service operations: manage incidents, monitor SLOs, and produce service reports
- Measure product outcomes: track adoption, feature utilization, and user satisfaction metrics
- Monitor AI/ML model health: detect drift, manage retraining triggers, produce AI monitoring reports
- Drive continuous improvement: maintain improvement backlog and raise change recommendations
- Prepare for Gate F governance review at the agreed periodic interval (recommended: every 6 months)

## Who
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Ops Lead | Service Reports, SLO monitoring, incident management |
| Product Lead | Product Analytics Reports |
| ML Lead | AI Monitoring Reports |
| PM + PO | Improvement Backlog, Change Recommendations |
| Steering Committee | Gate F decision (continue / pivot / retire) |

## Evidence Required for Gate F
| Artefact | Template | Evidence Level |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| Service Reports (current period) | `templates/phase-7/service-report.md.template` | Mandatory (reviewed) |
| AI Monitoring Reports | `templates/phase-7/ai-monitoring-report.md.template` | Conditional (if AI/ML in scope) |
| Improvement Backlog | `templates/phase-7/improvement-backlog.md.template` | Mandatory (reviewed) |
| Risk Register (current) | `templates/transversal/risk-entry.md.template` | Mandatory (reviewed) |

## Key Skills
- `metrics-tracking` — use when recording and reporting service metrics and AI monitoring data
- `ai-lifecycle` — use when managing model drift, retraining triggers, and AI ops monitoring
- `retrospective` — use when running continuous improvement review cycles
- `change-control` — use when evaluating and processing change recommendations
- `gate-checklist` — use when preparing the Gate F governance review package

## Entry Criteria
- Gate E passed; product live; operational baseline established

## Exit Criteria
- Gate F passed (continue decision) or retirement decision made; proceed to Phase 7 if retiring
