# Phase 7 — Retire or Replace Essentials

## What to Do
- Evaluate retirement triggers (end-of-life, replacement available, adoption collapse) and document the decision
- Assess impact on users, integrations, and data; produce Impact Assessment
- Produce Sunset Plan: communication, migration/deprecation timeline, data disposition
- Execute decommissioning: shut down infrastructure, execute data migration or deletion, close integrations
- Produce Final Closure Pack and obtain formal lifecycle close sign-off

## Who
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Steering Committee | Retirement Decision Record sign-off |
| PM | Impact Assessment, Sunset Plan, Final Closure Pack |
| PM + Engineering | Sunset Plan (joint) |
| Engineering Lead | Decommissioning Record |

## Evidence Required for Lifecycle Close
| Artefact | Template | Evidence Level |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| Lifecycle Closure | `templates/phase-7/lifecycle-closure.md.template` | Mandatory (approved) |

## Key Skills
- `artefact-authoring` — use when creating Retirement Decision Record, Sunset Plan, and Final Closure Pack
- `risk-management` — use when assessing and closing all open risks before decommissioning
- `metrics-tracking` — use when producing final product performance summary for the closure pack

## Entry Criteria
- Retirement trigger confirmed; Gate F passed or waived; decision made to retire or replace

## Exit Criteria
- All closure obligations met; Final Closure Pack signed off; lifecycle formally closed
